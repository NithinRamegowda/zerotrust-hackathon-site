About the Hackathon

The Zero Trust Hackathon is a collaborative initiative by Bank of Baroda, Skysecure and CyberFrat designed to accelerate innovation in cybersecurity for the financial sector. Participants will build prototypes across multiple tracks—Identity & Access, Data Protection, Endpoint Detection, AI for SOC Automation and Secure DevOps—while aligning to the Zero Trust principles.

Over nine weeks, teams will progress from idea submission through mentorship and prototype development to a live showcase at the Bank of Baroda headquarters on 29 September 2025. Successful projects will demonstrate realistic solutions that enhance trust, protect sensitive data, and streamline operations for banks.

Innovation Tracks

Identity & Access

Build solutions around Azure AD/Entra ID, conditional access policies and phishing-resistant multi‑factor authentication.

Data Protection & DLP

Implement data tagging, classification and leakage prevention using Microsoft Purview and Defender for Cloud Apps.

Endpoint Detection & Response

Integrate Microsoft Defender for Endpoint with attack simulations and threat intelligence feeds.

AI for SOC Automation

Utilise Azure OpenAI to automate alert correlation and incident summarisation, accelerating SOC L1 triage.

Secure DevOps

Integrate GitHub Advanced Security and Microsoft Defender for DevOps to build secure pipelines for financial applications.
